About

CloudStack Collaboration Conference is a 4-day virtual get together for the global CloudStack community and people interested in open-source technology.

The virtual event is aimed at developers, operators and users to discuss and evolve the open-source software project, its functionality and real-world operability. This event will allow you to meet the biggest CloudStack community, project members and contributors, and C-level executives from global MSPs, leading cloud providers, and IaaS builders.
